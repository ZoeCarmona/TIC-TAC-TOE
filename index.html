TIC-TAC-TOE

Tic-tac-toe is a game in which two players by turns put crosses and zeros in compartments of a figure formed by two vertical lines crossing two horizontal lines and each tries to get a row of three crosses or three zeros before the opponent does.

This game is a little unusual because instead of having a 3x3 gameboard, we have a 4x4 gamebord. But don´t worry, the rules exactly the same !!!!!
